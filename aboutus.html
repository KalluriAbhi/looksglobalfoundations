About Us

Looks Global Foundations is a leading Non-Profitable Organization in Hyderabad The organization works for needy people and the Health, Education of children to improve their life. It was set up in 2017, registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860. We strongly believe that Helping each other particularly people who are in need to build a happier society for everyone. It is not all about money - we can also give our time, ideas, energy and support for those who are doing something for a better society. Even with a very small help you can see a smile on someone’s face
