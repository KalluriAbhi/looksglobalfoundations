Company Culture

We are always looking out for passionate individuals to help make Vicare the world’s largest enterprise by revenue and impact.

Vicare combines the energy & agility of a start-up with the security & perks of an established enterprise to help each team member achieve their personal & professional potential. Business Development Manager

 Business Development Manager
• Employment ID: VIC001
• Experience – 0-1 Year
• Location – Hyderabad
• Job Type – Full-Time

Core Responsibilities
• Reaching out to prospects and guiding them through the existing investment opportunities
• Intensely following up with the prospects and closing the sales within the sales cycle.
• A committed attitude towards the completion of daily inputs and outputs, with the goal of matching (and exceeding) targets.
• Looking after the entire sales life cycle, starting from engaging with the large volume of well-qualified leads till the final conversion and subscription
• Ensuring accurate and proper record-keeping of call information in electronic and digital format.
• Achieving the monthly targets while completing all the input metrics targets.
• Be up to date with consumer and market trends to ensure better conversion.

Skills
• Expertise in business development(B2C)
• Proven ability to self-start, take initiative, work collaboratively, and drive results
• Proven track record of meeting/exceeding Sales targets.
• Possess excellent interpersonal as well as written and verbal communication skills.
• Must be a “go-getter” ready to take up tough challenges and have a never say die attitude.
• Basic understanding of equity markets
• Remain motivated and keep a positive attitude under pressure.
• Bachelor’s Degree in any field
