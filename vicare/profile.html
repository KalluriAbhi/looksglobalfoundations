About VICARE

VICARE is a leading financial and consulting firm headquartered in Hyderabad, India, renowned for its trusted services. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, we specialize in identifying and resolving complex challenges for our valued clients.

At VICARE, we understand that our role transcends mere business transactions. With an extensive asset, loan, equity, and mutual fund portfolio totaling nearly 10,000 Crores, backed by over 30 years of industry expertise, we uphold ethical and transparent practices in all our dealings. We prioritize integrity and honesty in our collaborations with partners, clients, and employees alike.


With a focus on delivering tailored solutions and fostering enduring relationships, VICARE is dedicated to empowering clients to achieve their financial objectives effectively and responsibly.

Core Offerings

VICARE Financial Services Certified professionals & Consultants with best industry experience of over 30 years, Vicare Team members have diverse skills, talents, and career experiences in delivering value towards your financial solutions and goals.
Financial Advisory & Consulting We believe that everyone deserves a financially secure future for themselves & their loved ones. Our wealth-building strategies are designed to do just that. With Vicare, you can develop the financial strength needed to make your life's dreams come true.
Tailored Services All our strategies and plans are built around an understanding of the global financial ecosystem and a deep appreciation of our clients’ needs and wants. Market out-performance and client success is at the heart of everything we do.
Client Satisfaction and Success We are only as successful as our customers. We design financial solutions to improve our customers’ lives and experiences. We listen to our customers to understand what they want and provide solutions for their company and individual goals.
